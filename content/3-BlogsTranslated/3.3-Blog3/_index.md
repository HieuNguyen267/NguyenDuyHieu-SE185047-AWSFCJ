# **New digital courses on AWS Graviton processors**

bySophy Chen June 30, 2025 in[Announcements](https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/training-and-certification/category/post-types/announcements/), [AWS Training and Certification](https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/training-and-certification/category/aws-training-and-certification/), [Gravity](https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/training-and-certification/category/compute/graviton/), [Permalink](https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/training-and-certification/new-digital-courses-about-aws-graviton-processors/).

The processors [AWS Graviton](https://aws.amazon.com/ec2/graviton/) Specially designed to deliver the best performance-to-cost ratio for cloud workloads running on the service [Amazon Elastic Compute Cloud (Amazon EC2)](https://aws.amazon.com/ec2/). Customers adopting Graviton have achieved up to 40% better performance per cost, while consuming up to 60% less power than comparable versions. Through this new course series, you will learn how to leverage Graviton to improve both application performance and the profitability of your business.

This series of three courses is designed to cover both the fundamentals and intermediate topics like migration and optimization. Whether you are a decision maker, developer, cloud architect, cloud operations specialist, DevOps engineer, or platform engineer, these courses are built to help you understand and leverage Graviton for your business.

## **Course details**

**Basic level:**

* [AWS Graviton Processor Fundamentals (30 min)](https://skillbuilder.aws/search?searchText=aws-graviton-processors-fundamentals&showRedirectNotFoundBanner=true): Learn the technical concepts and benefits of AWS Graviton processors, as well as the resources and support to apply them. You will also explore Amazon EC2 instances powered by Graviton processors to meet a variety of workload needs, delivering significant price-performance benefits and enhanced AI capabilities.

**Intermediate level:**

* [Migrate to AWS Graviton (2.5 hours)](https://skillbuilder.aws/search?searchText=migrating-to-aws-graviton&showRedirectNotFoundBanner=true): Learn how to assess application compatibility with AWS Graviton, build a comprehensive migration strategy, and use tools to assess migration readiness. You will gain a deep understanding of how both interpreted and compiled languages ​​operate on Graviton, including their unique behaviors and optimization techniques. You will also explore the differences between native, cross-compilation, and in-situ compilation strategies and learn how to apply them effectively. Finally, you will understand and build multi-architecture container images, deploy them on AWS Graviton-based infrastructure, and optimize container workloads with AWS Graviton.

* [Optimizing with AWS Graviton (40 mins)](https://skillbuilder.aws/search?searchText=optimization-with-aws-graviton&showRedirectNotFoundBanner=true): Explore different scenarios where AWS Graviton can provide significant advantages and learn how to leverage performance metrics and baseline optimization methods. Through real-world examples and best practices, you will be able to build effective optimization strategies and easily overcome common deployment pitfalls. Additionally, the course will guide you through optimizing .NET Core applications and Windows workloads on AWS Graviton.

## **Begin**

Enroll in these courses today and take the first step towards optimizing your cloud workloads with AWS Graviton. Happy learning\!
